The NP Bayes reading group is a reading group lead by Professor Wood in the Statistics Department at Columbia university. We meet weekly to discuss topics in machine learning and statistics, with a focus on the theory and practice of nonparametric Bayesian inference. The group meets from 1-2 pm on Thursday in room 1025 in the School of Social Work. If you are interested in joining, please contact either Professor Wood or David Pfau or check out our Google Group.
